Site Plan

Site Name

Simpsonville Area Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Simpsonville Area Chamber of Commerce site acts as a community platform for the growth of local business and advertise the local attractions of Simpsonville Area.

Scenarios

  • What local businesses are in Simpsonville currently?

  • What services does the local government provide and what is the contact information?

  • What are the top local attractions?

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?

  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?

  • What has been the population growth in the area?
